How to understand from the graphs that your valve body or solenoid is faulty? We know how.

Today there is a large number of test equipment for testing hydraulic systems of automatic transmissions. These stands can be divided into two large groups for testing solenoids and valve bodys. A distinctive feature of any such equipment is the presence of a professional operator. The results that such equipment produces must still be correctly interpreted and analyzed.

Operators who work with such equipment are expensive, and are a target for hunting. If the company decides to take a non-experienced specialist and prepare, then it is also not cheap, but also for a long time. Understanding all this, our team of engineers and programmers decided to create software for automatic analysis of test results. This will speed up the analysis process, eliminate the human factor and the need for a highly qualified specialist. If your company owns such equipment, then perhaps we can help you.
